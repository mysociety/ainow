TICTeC Local

Where Civic Tech meets Local Government

TICTeC Local is the annual conference from mySociety that focuses directly on tech for local government.

TICTeC Local was born from TICTeC, mySocietyâ€™s Impacts of Civic Tech conference. That annual international event brings together academics, activists, funders and tech NGOs from around the world share research into the impact of citizen-to-government initiatives.

TICTeC Local brings the same successful formula to Local Government in the UK.

Here's what to expect:

Presentations on the tech innovations and ideas that local authorities are putting to the test around the country.

Lively discussions on topics such as re-engaging disillusioned citizens, new directions in democracy, or tech that can drive efficiency.

Knowledge sharing with peers facing similar challenges in authorities up and down the UK.

Evidence-based research to help you evaluate the ideas and innovations under discussion.

Ongoing relationships with mySociety, other organisations and authorities. Take the opportunity to get involved in new concepts for councils at the discovery & prototyping phases; or form alliances and pool resources with peer authorities as you put your own ideas into practice.
