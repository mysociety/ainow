***TICTeC 2020 HAS BEEN CANCELLED***

As a result of the escalating impact of COVID-19 (coronavirus), we are sad to announce that we will be cancelling the 2020 edition of TICTeC (due to take place in Reykjavik on 24th and 25th March 2020). Please accept our sincere apologies for any inconvenience caused.

Although we won’t be hosting an in-person event, we are considering what form of virtual / remote conference sessions that we may be able to host. We will share further information on what we believe might be possible soon.

Everyone who has paid for a ticket for TICTeC 2020 will qualify for a full refund – we will be in touch with you about this shortly.
