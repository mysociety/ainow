TICTeC by mySociety

The global home of civic tech impact research

TICTeC stands for ‘The Impacts of Civic Technology Conference’. Since 2015, TICTeC has been an unmissable annual milestone, convening an international mix of researchers, practitioners, policymakers, philanthropists and tech giants to examine how civic technology is shaping society.

What makes TICTeC unique is its focus on high quality impact and research, with international leaders in the field sharing experiments, evidence and experiences from around the world. TICTeC is a safe place to examine what works, what doesn’t and how best this can be measured, so that better tools can be developed in future.

TICTeC is more than an annual event: it also promotes civic tech impact research and reflection in other ways, such as via the TICTeC Local conferences and the TICTeC Seminar series. Details of past and upcoming TICTeC events are below.
